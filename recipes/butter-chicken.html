Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani): a globally loved Indian curry, renowned for its rich, creamy texture and mildly spiced, flavorful gravy. This iconic dish features succulent pieces of marinated and grilled chicken (tandoori chicken is often used) simmered in a luscious tomato-based sauce, enriched with butter and cream. The harmonious blend of spices and the signature creamy finish make it an absolute delight, perfect for a cozy dinner or a special gathering. Discover the joys of preparing this classic at home!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Lemon Juice
  • Kashmiri Chili Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cashew Nuts
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Kasuri Methi
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps to make Butter Chicken

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Marinate boneless chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and a blend of spices.
  2. Cook the Gravy Base: Saut√© onions, ginger-garlic paste, and cashews until golden brown. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  3. Blend the Gravy: Blend the cooled mixture to a smooth puree.
  4. Sear the Chicken: Grill or pan-fry the marinated chicken until cooked and lightly browned.
  5. Assemble the Curry: In a pan, add butter and spices. Pour in the blended gravy and simmer.
  6. Combine and Finish: Add the cooked chicken, kasuri methi, and cream. Simmer until the flavors are well combined.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or basmati rice.
